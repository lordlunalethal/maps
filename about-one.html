Elton Mulenga (born 29 November 1994), widely known as Yo Maps, is a Zambian Afrobeat sensation whose music resonates with audiences across all walks of life. With a unique blend of storytelling and infectious rhythms, Yo Maps crafts songs that inspire the youth to embrace their culture, discover their potential, and dream of a brighter tomorrow. His lyrics encourage taking responsibility, valuing personal talents, and contributing positively to society, all while eradicating negativity and self-doubt. Yo Maps believes every note, every word, and every performance should uplift and empower others. Guided by the philosophy of connecting through music, Yo Maps transforms entertainment into a powerful tool for education and motivation, proving that a good beat can carry even greater lessons.
